How to use the 3D Map

Some information has been randomized to protect business critical information and the privacy of tenants

Network requests are being simulated to induce typical network latency

While this instruction set is significatly voluminous, understand that good money was paid for this project, all these features were necessary and had to be documented. blacked out units are sold while units in grey are reserved

reserved units are reserved for one of 2 reasons:

  • business decisions
  • said unit has been paid for by a tenant but contracts haven't been finalized clicking the search menu item reveals a sidebar containing an index of all shops currently in view
  • the status of each shop is visible
  • the names of the tenants of reserved/sold shops are also visible
  • the search bar can be used to find a shop unit if the shop number is known
  • clicking a shop unit from the 3D map also locates it in the sidebar, you can clear the search bar to restore the index of all visible shops

selection can be made using the checkboxes in the side bar or by SHIFT+clicking the interested units, as a rule of thumb, selections can't be made against sold units AND units that've been reserved for a tenant, for obvious reasons

the obvious reason being, you can't practically reserve/unreserve a sold unit or a unit that has been offered to someone else pending contract finalization

to allocate one or more shops, start by selecting available units, that is, units that aren't sold (BLACKED) or already reserved (GREY), when done with your selection, you may click the eye in the top left of the sidebar to toggle between viewing your active selection (for review processes) and all visible units, and then clicking the "Apply Selection" at the base of the list
